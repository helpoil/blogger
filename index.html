Migraine treatment at home

Ginger The best option for using ginger to get rid of migraine at home is to make ginger tea. You need to peel the ginger root, and cut it into small pieces. Add chopped ginger to boiling water, and simmer for about 10 minutes. After that, you can strain the resulting tea, and add lemon juice or honey to taste. Chamomile The best variety of this plant for getting rid of headaches and migraines is chamomile. Add two tablespoons of chamomile to a glass of water, and simmer the resulting mixture for 5 minutes. In addition, you can add buttercup and meadowsweet, or lemon juice with honey to give your tea an amazing taste. Lavender oil Dissolve a teaspoon of lavender oil in three cups of boiling water, and inhale for 15 minutes. In addition, you can mix lavender oil with peppermint oil, after which you should apply a small amount of oil to the temples and gently massage them to eliminate pain symptoms.
